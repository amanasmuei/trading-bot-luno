Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our trading bot

Is the trading bot really free?

Yes! Our trading bot is 100% free and open source. There are no subscription fees, hidden costs, or premium tiers. You get access to all features at no cost.

How secure is my API key and trading data?

Security is our top priority. Your API keys are stored locally on your machine and never transmitted to our servers. The bot uses read-only permissions where possible and implements bank-level encryption.

Can I customize the trading strategies?

Absolutely! Being open source, you have complete access to modify trading algorithms, risk parameters, and technical indicators. The code is well-documented for easy customization.

What trading pairs are supported?

We support 15+ trading pairs on Luno including BTC, ETH, LTC, and BCH against major fiat currencies like MYR, ZAR, EUR, GBP, NGN, and UGX.

Do I need programming knowledge to use it?

Not at all! We provide easy setup scripts, a user-friendly configuration wizard, and comprehensive documentation. Most users can get started in under 5 minutes.

Can I test strategies before using real money?

Yes! The bot includes a comprehensive simulation mode (paper trading) that lets you test strategies with real market data without risking actual funds.
